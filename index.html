About Us

PAATHSHALA ORGANISATION

Right to education is a fundamental right. Yet many children are deprived of education because schools are not available near their villages.

Paathshala Organisation aims to build schools in remote areas and provide free primary education for every child.

We also conduct awareness sessions on hygiene, sanitation, and the importance of education.

Your contribution can transform lives and help create brighter futures for generations.
