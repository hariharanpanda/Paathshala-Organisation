About Us

Education is a right, not a privilege.

PAATHSHALA ORGANISATION

Right to education is a fundamental right. Yet many children are still deprived of education because schools are not available near their villages.

Paathshala Organisation aims to build schools, provide learning resources, and create educational opportunities for underprivileged children.

We conduct awareness programs on hygiene, literacy, digital learning, and community development.

Our mission is to ensure that every child, regardless of background, receives access to quality education and a brighter future.
