About Us

PAATHSHALA ORGANISATION

Right to education is a fundamental right. Yet many children are deprived of education because schools are unavailable near their villages.

Paathshala Organisation aims to build schools in remote areas and provide free education for every child.

We also conduct awareness programs on hygiene, sanitation, and the importance of learning.

Your contribution helps us create brighter futures for generations to come.
